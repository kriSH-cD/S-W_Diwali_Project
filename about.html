About Us

Hello! I'm Krish Kumar Thakur, a passionate first-year student from Newton School of Technology, ADYPU Pune. I’m currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science (AI & ML) and have a strong interest in technology, innovation, and the beauty of nature.

I founded this platform — Mountain Hiking Adventures — with the goal of connecting people who love exploring mountains, nature, and adventure. This website serves as a digital space where explorers, trekkers, and hikers can discover trails, safety tips, hiking blogs, and breathtaking destinations across the world.

With a growing community of nature enthusiasts, our mission is to make mountain hiking more accessible, informative, and inspiring. Whether you're a beginner planning your first trek or a seasoned adventurer chasing new heights, this space is built for you.

Life's best views come after the hardest climbs.
